Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our mass spectrometry services.

General Questions

Q1: What types of mass spectrometry projects does ILS provides?

A1: Our institution provides various types of mass spectrometry projects, including metabolomics, proteomics, lipidomics, and small molecule analysis.

Q2: Do I need to have prior experience with mass spectrometry to use your facilities?

A2: No, our team is happy to assist users who may not have extensive experience with mass spectrometry. We offer training sessions and guidance to help you get started with your project.

Q3: Can I upload my own samples or do I need to purchase them through your ILS site?

A3: You can choose to either upload your own samples or purchase them through our online store. If you're unsure which option is best for your project, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Q4: How long does it take to receive results after submitting my sample(s) for analysis?

A4: The turnaround time for receiving results varies depending on the complexity of the project and the availability of our instruments. On average, we aim to deliver results within 7-14 business days.

Q5: Is my data secure when using your institution's facilities? Are there any confidentiality agreements in place?

A5: Yes, we take data security very seriously. All user data is stored securely behind firewalls and encrypted databases. Additionally, we require all users to sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) before accessing our facilities, ensuring the confidentiality of their research findings.
