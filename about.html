About Us

Advanced Mass Spectrometry Platform at ILS Bhubaneswar

The Institute of Life Sciences (ILS) Bhubaneswar is proud to present its Advanced Mass Spectrometry (MS) Facility, a state-of-the-art platform designed to enhance and support cutting-edge research in life sciences.

This facility represents a significant advancement in the analytical capabilities available to researchers at ILS and neighbouring institutions, providing access to high-resolution and highly sensitive mass spectrometry technologies.

Establishment and Support

The establishment of the Advanced MS Facility at ILS Bhubaneswar has been made possible through the generous support of the DBT SAHAJ-Infrastructure Programme. This initiative is part of the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of Indiaâ€™s efforts to bolster scientific infrastructure and promote advanced research capabilities across the country.

The DBT SAHAJ-Infrastructure Programme aims to enhance research facilities, foster innovation, and build research capacity by providing cutting-edge tools and technologies. With this support, the ILS MS Facility has been equipped to serve not only the researchers within ILS but also the faculties and research personnel from other institutions in Odisha and beyond.
