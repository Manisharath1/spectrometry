Services

Protein Identification

Utilizing high-resolution mass spectrometry, we identify proteins from complex biological samples with high accuracy and sensitivity.

Quantitative Proteomics

We offer label-free quantitation and various labeling techniques (TMT, SILAC) to measure protein abundance changes in different conditions.

Metabolomics

Comprehensive analysis of metabolites in biological samples to study metabolic pathways and biomarker discovery.

Targeted Proteomics

Quantitative analysis of specific proteins or peptides using selected reaction monitoring (SRM) or parallel reaction monitoring (PRM) techniques.

PTMs Analysis

Detection and characterization of PTMs such as phosphorylation, glycosylation, and ubiquitination to understand protein function and regulation.

Small Molecule Analysis

Identification and quantification of small molecules, including drugs, metabolites, and other organic compounds.

Peptide Mapping and Sequencing

Detailed mapping and sequencing of peptides for protein characterization and verification.

Bioinformatics Support

Comprehensive data analysis services, including database searches, quantitative analysis, and custom bioinformatics solutions.
