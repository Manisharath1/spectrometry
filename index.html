BRIC-ILS Mass Spectrometry Facility

Supported by the DBT SAHAJ-Infrastructure Program, our state-of-the-art facility is dedicated to advancing research and development in the field of mass spectrometry.

Explore More
About Us

The primary goal is to establish a cutting-edge Advanced Mass Spectrometry facility, which will serve as a vital resource for supporting the ongoing research endeavors of the Institute of Life Sciences (ILS) and neighboring academic institutions within Odisha.

Additionally, this initiative aims to facilitate capacity-building efforts through the provision of comprehensive workshops and hands-on training sessions for faculty members and research personnel from various institutions across Odisha, thereby enhancing their skills and expertise in the field of mass spectrometry.
